Steak

A well marbled beautiful cut of ribeye steak seared to perfect. Allow the ribeye to rest for an hour in room temperature. Then, sear it on a blazing hot cast iron pan with only kosher salt. Cook on each side for about 2 minutes. Baste with butter every 30 seconds

And...viola! A perfect steak.
